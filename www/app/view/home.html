About CurrencyConverter.com

Whether you need to convert your dollars into euros, pesos, or another currency, our Currency Conversion calculator gives you our real-time daily exchange rate. So you can see just how far your money will go and work out what a beer, coffee or sombrero will cost you! Simply choose the currency that you’re exchanging your US dollars for (or vice versa). We'll calculate the exchange rate for you and show you the conversion rate history – so you can see if it's a good time to buy.
